Cat Breeds

Persian

The Persian cat is a long-haired breed of cat characterized by its round face and short muzzle. Persians are known for their calm and gentle temperament.

Siamese

The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cat. Siamese cats are known for their slender bodies and blue almond-shaped eyes.
